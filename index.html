TDEC – Turner Decision Engine Computation

More than an indicator.

Private release. TDEC is a NinjaTrader 8 add-on built to answer one question in real, present-time market action:
“Is there market mover intention in this bar right now?”

If the bar shows real intention from the players who can actually move ES, TDEC speaks. If the bar is pretty but empty, TDEC stays quiet. That selectiveness is the edge.

The problem

Markets don’t move just because there was news. Markets move because someone with size intended to move it. That’s why the same headline can send price up, down, or nowhere.

Most traders are still trading indirect coincidences to past movement — moving averages, oscillators, calendar cycles, old ranges, even cute Delta (a program based on the moon and tides) theories. Those can be right once and then completely wrong on the very next day because the second move had no mover intention behind it. The market doesn’t care about cute timing. It moves when real money intends to move it.

Indicators talk from the rear-view mirror. TDEC judges the present.

What TDEC is

TDEC is a computational decision engine that inspects the live bar and evaluates the relationship between effort and result.

On every 1-second ES bar it asks:

  • Significance – is this bar big enough to matter to professionals
  • Directional efficiency – did effort actually push price, or was it expensive and sloppy
  • Context – do the surrounding bars support the conclusion of intention or was it a fake

If those align, TDEC prints. If they don’t, TDEC stays silent. Silence is a feature — it kept output to about 1.4 signals per day in real market action, which is where a human can actually execute.

Why it wins

  • Present-time, not pattern-time. Decides on this bar, not what used to work.
  • Effort/result aware. Sees impressive-looking bars that actually cost too much.
  • Selective. Most indicators talk too much; TDEC only talks when market mover intention is visible.
  • Context-friendly. In the hands of a trader who can see building or diminishing power, it becomes an ongoing edge, not a one-day trick.

Who it’s for

TDEC is for experienced ES / futures traders who already know structure and just want a present-time judge of whether the push they are seeing is real.

It is not for people who want magic arrows on 5-minute charts. It is for traders who can look at a TDEC print and say, “Given this context, that’s tradable.”
